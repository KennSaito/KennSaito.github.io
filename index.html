Welcome!

Welcome to my ePortfolio! Here you will find the different projects I have worked on throughout the year in New Media and Tech for Social Change. These projects gave us an understanding of genetics and the implications of it on race theory, allowed us to express our ideas through screenprinting, and put our inference skills to the test to recreate a mysterious mechanism. The links above will bring you to pages explaining each project and what I learned. Enjoy!

How This Website Was Made

I made this website using the languages HTML and CSS on a platform called GitHub. I created a repository and added pages to serve as my website, using a style.css file to decorate it. Whenever I was confused, I would use the website W3Schools, a website with many resources to help you understand and use certain elements and properties. My favicon, the icon for the website tab, was made in Adobe Photoshop and is inspired by the fictional character Clarence from the show Clarence.
